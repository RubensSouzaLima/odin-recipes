Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is both a type of pasta made up of wide ribbons, as well as a dish, sometimes called baked lasagna, made with these ribbons placed in layers, and interspersed with filling (cheese, ham, ground meat or others) and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Add the tomato sauce
  3. On a platter add a layer of dough
  4. Add ham and cheese
  5. Add another layer of sauce
  6. Repeat the previous steps until the dish is complete
